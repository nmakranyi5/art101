Hello! My name is Nikolas Makranyi, and I am a third year student double majoring in Computer Science and Computer Science: Game Design at UCSC. I am from the San Francisco Bay Area, and have lived here my whole life.

I am interested in all things tech related. Being a student here at UCSC, I have been exposed to many different aspects of tech, ranging from using Assembly Language, to running Machine Learning algorithms on datasets. On the side, I also enjoy designing games, as I like coming up with ideas for new universes and settings. Games I have made include Astrojumper, an Asteroids-esque space shooter made on GDevelop, and Stormheir: Retribution, a visual novel game made with Twine.

Other than tech related topics, I enjoy activities like working out at the gym, kayaking, and just going on walks. I also sometimes dabble in making music, which is pretty fun to do. Writing stories is also a hobby of mine, as this allows me to drift off to another world. I also enjoy playing video games, and the game I am currently playing is The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt.

A fun fact about me is that I have a cat named Simba, who is 7 years old.
